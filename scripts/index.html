ml-tree-similarity explorer

Toggle the matrix algorithm between the current center-of-mass tree and the equal-integration vector scored with ml-spectra-processing (xEqualIntegrationVectorSimilarity); green = high similarity, pink/red = low. Click a cell to compare two spectra, or a spectrum name (or column number) to rank every sample by similarity to it — then click a ranked row to compare the two spectra. Hover a name for its description. On the plots: drag to zoom X (synchronized), double-click to reset, scroll wheel to zoom Y. The orange tree in the plots follows the selected algorithm — center-of-mass splits from createTree, or the equal-integration split vector; the yellow line marks zero intensity.
